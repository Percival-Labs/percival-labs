### RECITALS

WHEREAS, Assignor is the sole inventor of certain inventions described in a provisional patent application titled "TRUST-STAKED ECONOMIC ACCOUNTABILITY SYSTEM FOR AUTONOMOUS AI AGENTS WITH PRIVACY-PRESERVING TRUST ATTESTATION AND PER-AGENT INFERENCE GOVERNANCE" (the "Invention"); and

WHEREAS, Assignor desires to assign to Assignee all right, title, and interest in and to the Invention and any patent applications and patents resulting therefrom;

---

### AGREEMENT

**1. Assignment.** Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee, its successors, and assigns, the entire right, title, and interest in and to:

(a) The Invention as described in the provisional patent application;

(b) Any and all provisional, non-provisional, continuation, continuation-in-part, divisional, and reissue patent applications that may be filed based on or claiming priority from the provisional patent application, in the United States and in all foreign countries;

(c) Any and all patents that may be granted on the above applications;

(d) Any and all rights to claim priority under the Paris Convention, the Patent Cooperation Treaty, or any other international agreement;

(e) Any and all causes of action for infringement of any of the foregoing, including the right to sue for and collect damages for past, present, and future infringement.

**2. Cooperation.** Assignor agrees to execute all documents, take all actions, and provide all assistance reasonably requested by Assignee to perfect the assignment, prosecute patent applications, and enforce patent rights, at Assignee's expense.

**3. Consideration.** This assignment is made in consideration of Assignor's membership interest in Assignee (Percival Labs LLC) and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged.

**4. Representations.** Assignor represents that:

(a) Assignor is the sole inventor of the Invention;

(b) Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, or encumbered any rights in the Invention;

(c) Assignor has full right and authority to make this assignment;

(d) To the best of Assignor's knowledge, the Invention does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

**5. Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
