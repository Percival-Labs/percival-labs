# Percival Labs -- Instagram Content Playbook

**Platform:** Instagram (Carousels + Reels)
**Brand Voice:** Sage + Rebel + Creator -- a builder who uses AI, not an AI expert who builds
**Audience:** Regular people -- tradespeople, parents, small business owners. NOT developers.
**Color Palette:** Cyan (#00D4FF) + Amber (#FFB800) on Dark (#0A0E1A)
**Date:** February 22, 2026

---

## Quick Reference: Brand Voice Rules

**DO:**
- Talk like a builder who happens to use AI
- Use carpentry metaphors and building analogies
- Share real numbers, real failures, radical transparency
- Use "we" and "us" -- community, not product
- Reference the physical world

**NEVER SAY:**
- Revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive
- Unlock, leverage (as verb)
- Corporate third person ("Percival Labs is pleased to...")
- Fear-based selling ("AI will replace you if...")

**PRODUCTS:**
1. **Engram** -- Open-source personal AI infrastructure (~36K tokens, auditable, portable)
2. **Vouch** -- Nostr-native trust staking for AI agents (SDK on npm, API on Railway)
3. **The Lab / Terrarium** -- Visual agent workspace showing 6 AI agents working in real-time
